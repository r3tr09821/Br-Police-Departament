Case #1988-109: 7 Missing Children

STATUS: ACTIVE INVESTIGATION

Location: Tractorul neighborhood, Bra»ôov

Last seen: Central Park, May 3, 1988, around 18:30

Case Details

  • 4 children (ages 7-12) disappeared while playing together near NEVER LAND Circus.
  • Witnesses report seeing the group near the circus and snack kiosk before they vanished.
  • No signs of struggle or suspicious activity were observed in the area.
  • Security cameras show the children entering the circus but do not capture their exit.
  • Parents reported the children missing after they failed to return home by 19:00-20:00.
  • Police have conducted searches in the park and surrounding neighborhoods with no results so far.
  • Local volunteers and canine units are assisting in the search.
  • Anyone with information is urged to contact Brookvale Police Department immediately.

Children Information

  • Emily Johnson (Age 8)
  • Michael Brown (Age 9)
  • Sarah Williams (Age 10)
  • Noah Mitchell (Age 12)
